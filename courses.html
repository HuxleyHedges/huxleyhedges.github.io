Huxley Hedges


Courses

We are able to hold courses and outdoor ‘Team Building’ days at £75/head. Huxley Hedges has been running courses for both individual participants and organisations, including TCV, over the past 20 years. Whether you are a beginner or have some experience, the course experience will adapt to your needs. Tools are provided but gloves and sturdy boots are required.
Our courses are usually one day and allow people to get a taste for hedgelaying and working outdoors. You are able to learn more about hedgelaying and its importance as well as see the skill involved in laying a hedge.
A day of outdoor activity has a positive effect on mental health and physical wellbeing and partaking on a course allows you to meet people you share similar outdoor interests with.
For more information on attending a course or holding a course contact us on huxleyhedges88@gmail.com
