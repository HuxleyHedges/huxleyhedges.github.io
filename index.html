Benefits of Hedgelaying

Having a hedge laid instead of using fencing or just allowing a hedge to grow has a number or benefits.

It makes for a longer lasting hedge as the cuts allow for new growth throughout the hedge, the new growth also creates a thicker bushier hedge. This means a richer and more diverse habitat for a number of important insects, birds and mammals.

A well laid hedge can also prevent soil erosion and capture pollutants.
