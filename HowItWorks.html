Have a look through some informational videos filmed by Alison that will help you understand how we work on a hedge, solve any problems that arise, tackle any problems and the benfits of hedgelaying

In this video Alison discusses how we tackle a gappy hedge through laying

In this video, Alison describes and demonstrates how a hedge can be laid with a chainsaw, a time-saving technique better suited for those not familiar with billhooks and axes. Please only attempt what is shown in the video if you have relevant training and experience with a chainsaw. Ensure you only use a chainsaw on suitably sized hedges, nothing too tall or too heavy that you cannot manage on your own.

In this video Alison shows you how to lay a hedge with a billhook, taking you through what to look and listen out for. Remember that a billhook is double-edged so please use it carefully and sensibly.
